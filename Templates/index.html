About SimulatorX

SimulatorX is a product of the research project titled "Epidemic Spread Prediction Using Facial Recognition And Distancing Systems". The software mainly focuses on epidemic simulations through network theory considering biological cues like age, gender, and ethnicity of the target population. Three epidemiological models are supported for simulations: SI, SIS, and SIR. Further, it also provides flexibility to switch network graphs.

What's new in SoftwareX?

The software uses facial recognition system to extract three biological characteristics: age, gender, and ethnicity. These characteristics are then used to simulate through the disease network and find the prediction of chances to get infected by the disease.
